## About

I am a computational physicist and doctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for the Structure and Dynamics of Matter (MPSD) and DESY in Hamburg. My research focuses on magnetic nanostructures, with particular emphasis on skyrmion stability, coupled dynamics, and resonant excitations in synthetic antiferromagnets and thin-film systems.

I develop reproducible, open-source scientific software and contribute to the Ubermag ecosystem. My work emphasises computational rigour, software testing, and transparent research practices.
